Cherry Peak Ski Resort

Cherry Peak is Utah's newest ski resort. Located near Logan, Utah.

Utah's 15th ski resort will be opening for the 2014/15 ski season, featuring: + Snow making system + 3 triple chair lifts + 500 Foot magic carpet for learning + 5 lane tubing hill + Ice skating + Large night skiing area + Mountain biking + 3.7 miles from downtown Richmond + 15 miles from Logan + Group discounts available + Open Mon - Sat: 9 am - 9 pm
