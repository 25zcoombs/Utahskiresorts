Brian Head Ski Resort

Brian Head Resort is the place you should be skiing regardless of where you live! Great snow, great prices, and uncrowded slopes are closer than you think.

With an average 360 inches of annual snowfall, 2 high speed quad lifts, 6 fixed grip lifts, 3 surface conveyors, 71 runs and 650 acres of the most beautiful skiing and riding in the west, Brian Head Resort is your top Southern Utah ski destination. Beautiful views of red rock from this high desert alpine environment make for an unforgettable experience, winter or summer. An easy three-hour drive north out of Las Vegas on Interstate 15 makes Brian Head Resort a quick get-away from the Entertainment Capital of the World. Flights to and from Las Vegas are available from all major metropolitan areas within the U.S.. Flights are also available from SLC International to Cedar City, only 32 miles away! You can easily access our snowy playground with unforgettable skiing and hometown hospitality from anywhere in the US. Thanks to our low pass prices and the area's overall affordability, Brian Head Resort is also a popular destination for skiers and boarders from Southern California, Phoenix and Flagstaff - all within an easy 6-8 hour drive.

In addition to Brian Head Resort's 71 runs and progressive terrain parks, there are plenty of additional winter amenities and activities in the area, including: snow tubing, cross-country skiing, snowmobiling, snowshoeing and more. Dining, shopping, and day spa amenities are just down the street. Zion or Bryce Canyon National Parks are easily accessed from Brian Head Resort as both parks are less than 90 miles away
